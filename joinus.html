JOIN US





We function by selling art throughout the country, comicons, art-stores, you name it. Here at Artists Against Cancer, we function based on the contributions of the kind artists who help us. If you believe your talents to be worthy of selling, please do the following or fill out the registration form:

  1. Please reach us at Artistsagainstcancer7@gmail.com
  2. Please include your name, specialty and how long it takes you to finish a work
  3. Also please attatch at least two images of your past works
  4. If you are accepted, and looking for art supplies we have an arangement with the artarama store, check it out for special discounts!

We truly appreciate every one of you, and though not everyone may be able to work with us, we thank you nonetheless!
