It all started with an idea...










In 2025

We had an idea:

What if we could help? It was a difficult thing to ask of ourselves, after all, we were so small and cancer was so big. But we realized that working together we were much stronger than cancer would ever be. But HOW? How to stop cancer? I know, tough question. But we realized something; if cancer is something made of destruction, we should fight it with it's polar opposite, construction, imagination, and of course ART. That's why we founded Artists Against Cancer. We make and sell art to regular folks like you, where 100% of the money goes to cancer research, that way, you too can make a difference. We hope you'll join us in our fight, to stop cancer with our ultimate weapon, art.
